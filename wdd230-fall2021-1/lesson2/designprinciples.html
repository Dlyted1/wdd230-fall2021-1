Design Principles

Kathleen Moore

Contrast

Dominos Pizza

www.dominos.com

Domino's Pizza includes a nice contrast on their home page. The colors of red, blue and white really work together on standing out which makes the reader take interest. The information on this page is also easy to understand with the use of image placements.

Alignment

World Equestrian Center

www.worldequestriancenter.com

All the visual elements and text boxes here repeat themselves giving a nice clean look to the overall page. Everything seems connected through the alignment in this web site.

White Space

Chewy

www.chewy.com

Chewy uses a lot of white space throughout the home page. With lots of catagories to shop by the white space makes it easier to focus on these elements. The result is a good user experience.
