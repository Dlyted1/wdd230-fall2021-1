Purpose

The main purpose of this web site is to forcast the current weather for equestrians and their horseback riding activities. Whether it's a trail ride, outside training or entering a horse show this site will forcast the current weather conditions to plan accordingly.

Site Name

The site will be called skyequestrian.net relating to what is happening in the sky regarding the weather at the time of the viewers equestrian event.
