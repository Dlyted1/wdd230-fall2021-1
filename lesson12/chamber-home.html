Welcome

Our mission is to serve the economic, educational, governmental and social interests of the people, organizations and communities it represents while fostering a prosperous and harmonious environment in which to live, learn and work.


Since its inception in 1925, the Greater Sebring Chamber of Commerce has been a member supported, member exclusive, grassroots, private business organization. The Chamber is led by a Board of Directors comprised of member business leaders and a Chamber President/CEO who acts under the direction and oversight of the board.
